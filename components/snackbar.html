Snackbar

Snackbar display messages related to an interaction happened between user and the website. They usually stay on the screen for 2-3 seconds and then disappear. They can be used to show success messages, warnings or errors.

Standard Snackbar

It is a normal snackbar having actions inline.

Can't send photo. Try in 5 seconds!
RETRY

Stacked Snackbar

It is a stacked version of snackbar having full width actions stacked below the display messages.
